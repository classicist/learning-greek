Activities

At the moment, this site is a small collection of surveys and brief assessments (collectively called Activities). Don’t expect to learn too much at the moment—we’re focused on building the platform and gathering some initial data. Over time there will be more learning experiments designed to actually teach new things. We’re laying a foundation first.

Because so much of this site is experimental, we want to allow for different tolerances people may have for being on the “bleeding edge”. For this reason, you can go to Settings in the top right nav bar and set your Adoption Level. By default you are considered an Early Adopter which means you will be shown activities that have been tried out by at least 10 people already. If your tolerance for new, un-tested stuff is higher, you might consider changing your setting to Bleeding Edge which gives you immediate access (so there may be problems). If your tolerance is even lower than the default, you can set it to Mainstream which means 100 people have to already have tried an activity before you can. Note that this means it may be a long time before activities are available to you. But it’s up to you.
